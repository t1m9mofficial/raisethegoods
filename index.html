About Raise The Goods

Raise the Goods is a brand new TV show that gives emerging Irish nonprofits, social enterprises and charities a platform to showcase an innovation and pitch to philanthropists for a share in €1,000,000.

Raise the Goods is a chance to get a financial leg-up and exposure to a national audience.

How it Works

Apply

New and innovative entrepreneurs, nonprofits and social enterprises submit an entry:

  • Tell us your origin story.
  • Tell us where you're heading.
  • And tell us how a share in €1,000,000 will make a difference to your work.

Finalists Announced

After a review of all submissions, 16 finalists will be announced to be part of the first season of the Raise The Goods

Television Event

Finalists will participate in Raise The Goods television show. Showcase their enterprise and compete to pitch to philanthropists.

Winners Announced

Four winners will be announced in the final episode.
